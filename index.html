Introduction

Welcome to the Couples Schema Questionnaire. This assessment helps identify relationship patterns based on schema therapy principles.

Instructions:

  • The questionnaire consists of 100 questions across 10 schema categories.
  • For each statement, indicate how much you agree or disagree using the scale provided.
  • Answer based on your current relationship or your most significant past relationship.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. Be as honest as possible for accurate results.
  • Your responses will help identify relationship patterns that may be affecting your relationship satisfaction.

Rating Scale:

0 Disagree
1 Neither agree nor disagree
2 Slightly agree
3 Agree
4 Strongly agree
